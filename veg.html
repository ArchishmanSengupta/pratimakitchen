  7. 75 ripe tomatoes, finely chopped

NANPURI

INGREDIENTS

  1. 2 Chicken legs
  2. 1 cup Hung Curd (Greek Yogurt)
  3. 1 Dry Red Chilli
  4. 2 Bay leaves (tej patta)
  5. 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini)
  6. 1 Onion , ground to paste
  7. 2 inch Ginger , grated or paste
  8. 5 cloves Garlic , minced
  9. 2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder
  10. 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi)
  11. 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder
  12. 1-1/2 tablespoon Cashew nuts , ground to paste
  13. 3 tablespoons Poppy seeds , soaked in warm water for 20 minutes
  14. Kewra Water , a few drops
  15. Ghee , or butter as required for cooking
  16. Salt , to taste
  17. 1 pinch Sugar
